Become a Mini Giant today!

Auskick is the Junior program of Australian Rules Football. It is open to any children from age 2 – 17 and includes skills practice, games and lots of fun. We are on the verge of building up a children (age 2-11) team on the one hand and a youth (age 12-17) one on the other hand. Please get in contact if you are interested.
